About Us

Our Story & Commitment to Quality

Madan

Founder & Professional Beverage Maker

Welcome to Natural Badam Milk

A newly launched beverage startup founded by Madan, a professional beverage maker with special expertise in crafting rich and healthy Badam (almond) milk.

With years of hands-on experience, Madan brings a perfect blend of tradition, taste, and quality to every drink. At Natural Badam Milk, we specialize in customized beverages tailored to match each customer's unique preferences.

Our Signature Flavors

  • Kesar Badam (Saffron Almond)
  • Rose Badam
  • Cinnamon Badam
  • Chocolate Badam
  • Butterscotch Badam
  • Kesar Pista Badam (Saffron, Pistachio, Almond)

Our Commitment

All our beverages are prepared using high-quality natural ingredients, without any artificial flavors or preservatives. We follow strict hygiene and cleanliness standards in every step of our preparation. From sourcing fresh ingredients to maintaining a sanitized kitchen environment, your health and safety are our top priorities.

Our pricing is based on current market value, ensuring affordable rates without compromising on quality or hygiene.
